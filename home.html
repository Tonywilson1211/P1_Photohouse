About Us!
. . .

Specialists in London Property Market

Photohouse was founded in 2012 from a passion to create the best marketing material and services for the London Property Market. Our aim was to improve the quality of photography available across the market and provide high end magazine ready photos and floorplans for every property we visit. We wanted to show that this could all be done with a friendly and professional attitude so that every party involved enjoyed the process.

Passion and Attention to Detail

Passion and attention to detail has been the key to our success and continued growth in becoming the preferred supplier for London’s estate agents. Photohouse offers marketing services to Estate Agents, Interior Designers, Architects and Developers throughout London. The company’s Headquarters is in Putney, South West London, from here we run our team of creative photographers, meticulous floor planners and Energy Performance Assessors.
