Our Services

Photography

At Photohouse our photography speaks for itself! We treat every photo shoot individually and concentrate on capturing the highest quality images possible from every location we visit. Our photography team is made up of highly experienced individuals who pride themselves on achieving our level of quality using the best equipment available. We are all extremely passionate about our photography and this shows in the finished product. We believe that a property should have total visual coverage and capture what makes the property unique and individual.

Floorplans

Accuracy is key in an industry where every bit of space is important we confidently use the latest Leica laser measuring technology to make sure that our floorplans are clear and precise. We supply both residential and commercial floorplans and leaseplans for a wide range of marketing uses. Floorplans are a crucial tool within advertising any space whether it be residential or commercial. Our team are highly trained and have a vast wealth of experience across the residential and commercial sector. We work closely with our CAD drawing team to ensure accuracy and full RICS compliance. Our floorplans can be tailored to any style, colour and branding.

EPCs

Our energy assessors provide accurate and thorough Energy Performance Certificates (EPCs), which if you are selling or letting your property are now mandatory to obtain. We offer a prompt and efficient turnaround time, working with you to get your property on the market as quickly as possible. An EPC is performed by an assessor who visits the property, examines key items such as loft insulation, the domestic boiler, hot water tanks, radiators and windows. The finished package will help advise homeowners on how to minimize energy loss and save money in the long term.
